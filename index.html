What We Do

Concrete Flooring. Every Surface.

From residential polished floors to large-scale commercial coatings — we have the experience and systems to deliver exceptional results.

Polished Concrete

High-gloss or matte polished concrete systems for residential, commercial, and industrial spaces. Durable, stunning, and low maintenance.

Grind & Seal

Grind and seal systems that enhance your existing concrete with a protective coating. Great value, great finish.

Concrete Surface Prep

Professional surface preparation including grinding, scarifying, and shot blasting — ensuring coatings bond correctly every time.

U3 Leveling

Precision floor leveling for uneven substrates. We prepare your concrete to perfect tolerance before any coating or polish system.

Block Honing

Expert honing of concrete blocks, pavers, and masonry surfaces to achieve a smooth, refined finish for modern homes and builds.

Acid Washing

Acid washing to clean, etch, and prepare concrete surfaces — removing contaminants and opening pores for optimal coating adhesion.
